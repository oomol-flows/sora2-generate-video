# Sora2 Video Generator

Transform your ideas into stunning videos with the power of AI. This toolkit makes video creation as simple as describing what you want to see or starting with an image you already have.

## What Can You Create?

With this toolkit, you can bring your imagination to life in two exciting ways:

### Turn Words into Videos

Simply describe what you want to see, and watch it come alive as a video. Whether you're imagining a peaceful beach at sunset, a bustling city street, or a fantasy landscape, just write it down and the AI will create it for you.

Perfect for:
- Visualizing story ideas before writing
- Creating unique content for social media
- Exploring creative concepts without expensive production
- Generating video clips for presentations
- Experimenting with visual storytelling

### Animate Your Images

Have a favorite photo or illustration? Give it motion and life! Describe how you want the scene to move or change, and your still image transforms into a dynamic video clip.

Great for:
- Making photo memories more engaging
- Adding motion to artwork or designs
- Creating eye-catching content from existing images
- Bringing historical photos to life
- Enhancing marketing materials with movement

## Customize Your Videos

You have full control over how your videos look:

**Size and Shape**: Choose portrait format (9:16) for social media stories and mobile viewing, or landscape (16:9) for traditional video content and presentations.

**Quality**: Select 720p for faster creation and smaller files, or 1080p for crystal-clear, high-quality results.

**Length**: Create quick 4-second clips for attention-grabbing moments, 8-second videos for social media posts, or 12-second sequences for more detailed scenes.

## How They Work Together

These two tools complement each other beautifully:

- Start with text to generate an initial scene, then use that video as inspiration to create variations with the image tool
- Create a video from text, extract a frame you love, and animate it in a different direction
- Use the text tool for original concepts and the image tool to continue or extend existing visuals
- Combine multiple short clips created from different descriptions to tell a longer story

## What You'll Receive

After creating your video, you'll get:

- The finished video file, ready to download and use
- A web link to share your creation online
- A preview image thumbnail
- Video details (dimensions, length, frame rate) for your reference

## Real-World Examples

**Travel Content Creator**: Describe "aerial view of ancient temple at sunrise with golden light" to create establishing shots for travel videos, even before visiting the location.

**Educator**: Bring historical moments to life by animating old photographs with descriptions like "soldiers marching through muddy trenches" to help students visualize history.

**Social Media Manager**: Transform product photos into engaging videos by describing desired motion: "camera slowly orbits the product while light gleams off its surface."

**Writer**: Visualize scenes from your story by describing them, helping you better communicate your vision to readers or collaborators.

Start with simple descriptions and experiment with different settings to discover what works best for your creative vision.
